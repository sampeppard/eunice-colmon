CAREER TRANSITIONING


OVERVIEW

Do you have ambitions and dreams? Questions? Doubts about your future? You owe it to yourself to get answers. Whether you are a new grad, entry- to mid-level professional, executive, or career-changer in transition, hear these words: Stop 'clicking' the mouse and struggling at home, alone. Your health, happiness, and financial freedom depend on it.

Technology, globalization, and change continue to drive employer requirements and increase demands on you. How can you improve your chances in today's competitive market? Career Coaching!

Through personalized coaching you can change your perspective, learn progressive job searching strategies, and take control of your career success—despite external ‘matters and chatters.


Process & Experience

No matter where you are on your journey, we'll start by clarifying your current skills, interests, values, personality traits, and career direction. From there, we'll assess your marketing strategy and develop the tools you'll need to compete. Eunice motivates and guides you through a proven process, one step at a time, so you can achieve the results you desire.


Benefits & Outcomes

With top-notch self-marketing tools and effective communication strategies, you will have:

  • ✔ Clearer understanding of who you are, what you want, and what you offer
  • ✔ Ability to distinguish yourself from other candidates
  • ✔ Power to wage a well-organized, tailored job search campaign
  • ✔ Confidence and skill to engage in the interview process from start to finish
  • ✔ Increased negotiating power!


CAREER COACHING PACKAGES

Discover your passion and achieve your vision through a Career Coaching package customized specifically for you:



CAREER DIRECTION – 4 Phases


  1. 1. Initial Consultation (1 hour)
  2. 2. Customized Assessments (e.g., Meyers-Briggs Type Indicator, Strong Interest Inventory)
  3. 3. Interpretation Consultation (1½ - 2 hours)
  4. 4. Follow-up Consultation (1 hour)


RÉSUMÉ DEVELOPMENT – 3 Phases


  1. 1.Initial Consultation (1 hour)
  2. 2. Résumé Creation
  3. 3.Editing & Finalization Consultation (1 hour)


COVER LETTER DEVELOPMENT & TUTORIAL– 3 Phases


  1. 1. Internalizing assessment results (e.g., DISC, Meyers-Briggs Type Indicator, Social Styles)
  2. 2. Coaching direct reports on leadership
  3. 3. Establishing clear roles and responsibilities for direct reports
  4. 4. Aligning priorities with departmental strategies
  5. 5. Driving change with vision, purpose, and patience
  6. 6. Applying advanced management and leadership skills
  7. 7. Managing Up – Defining, accepting, and meeting manager’s (sponsor’s) expectations
  8. 8. Managing anger, disappointment, and expectations; Increasing emotional intelligence


ELEVATOR PITCH EXECUTION – 3 Phases


  1. 1. Initial Consultation (30-45 minutes)
  2. 2. Elevator Pitch Review and Editing (30 minutes)
  3. 3. Elevator Pitch Practice (30 minutes)


LINKEDIN PROFILE DEVELOPMENT– 3 Phases


  1. 1. Initial Consultation (30-45 minutes)
  2. 2. Profile Creation
  3. 3. Editing & Final Consultation (30-45 minutes)


INTERVIEW PREPARATION – 3 Phases


  1. 1. Résumé vs. Job Posting Assessment / Protocol Review (1 hour)
  2. 2. Q&A Practice and Feedback Consultation (1½ hours)
  3. 3. Mock Interview and Debrief (2 hours)


DECISION-MAKING/SALARY NEGOTIATION/ACCEPTANCE – 3 Phases


  1. 1. Values and Needs vs. Job Offer Discussion (1 hour)
  2. 2. Negotiation Strategy Preparation (1 hour)
  3. 3. ‘First 30 Days’ Planning (1 hour)
